What is GesConnect?

GesConnect is our idea for an android app that takes in gestures, analyzes them, and quickly give you access to
several different features for your phone. Our aim was to make GesConnect a drawing board shortcut app for your phobe
that simplifies several different interactions between the user and their phone. GesConnect can give you access to your
contacts, messages, search bar, and more all at the simple flick of the wrist! Our application also offers a handy
custom gesture feature that allows you to associate anything you can draw with any person on your contact list.
